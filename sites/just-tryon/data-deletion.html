App Information

App Name: Just Try-On

Developer: EJS Studios

Package Name: com.ejsstudios.virtualoutfit

Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data while keeping your Just Try-On account active. This gives you greater control over your personal information while maintaining access to the app.

Steps to Request Data Deletion:

  1. In-App Method (Recommended):
    • Open the Just Try-On app
    • Go to Profile → Account Management
    • Tap "Delete My Data"
    • Review what will be deleted
    • Confirm your decision in the dialog
    • Your data will be deleted immediately
  2. Email Request:
    • Send an email to: support@ejsstudios.com
    • Subject: "Data Deletion Request - Just Try-On"
    • Include your registered email address
    • Specify which data you want deleted (or all data)
    • We will process your request within 7 business days

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Generated Images: All AI-generated virtual try-on images stored locally and in cloud
  • AI Model Configurations: Custom AI model settings, API keys, and preferences
  • App Preferences: Theme settings, default configurations, and user preferences
  • Usage Statistics: Generation counts, streaks, activity data, and usage analytics
  • Cached Data: Temporary files, image cache, and processing data
  • Custom Settings: Default model photos, quick try configurations

✅ Data That Will Be Preserved:

  • Profile Information: Name, email address, profile picture
  • Account Authentication: Google Sign-In association and login credentials
  • Basic Account Settings: Account creation date and basic preferences

Your account remains active and you can continue using the app with a fresh start.

⚠️ Important Notice:

  • Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • All generated images will be permanently lost
  • AI model configurations will need to be set up again
  • Usage statistics and progress will be reset
  • Your account remains active for continued use

Selective Data Deletion

You can also request deletion of specific types of data:

  • Images Only: Delete all generated images while keeping configurations
  • AI Models Only: Remove AI model settings while keeping images
  • Statistics Only: Reset usage data while keeping content

Contact us via email to request selective data deletion with specific requirements.

Data Retention Policy

Upon data deletion request:

  • Immediate: Data is removed from active app usage
  • Within 7 days: Data is removed from our servers
  • Within 30 days: Data is permanently deleted from all backups
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

After Data Deletion

After your data is deleted:

  • You can continue using the app normally
  • You'll need to reconfigure AI models and preferences
  • Previous generations and statistics will not be recoverable
  • You can start fresh with new content and settings
