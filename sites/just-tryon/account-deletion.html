Request Account Deletion

If you wish to permanently delete your Just Try-On account and all associated data, please follow the steps below. This action is irreversible and will permanently remove all your information from our systems.

Steps to Request Account Deletion:

  1. In-App Method (Recommended):
    • Open the Just Try-On app
    • Go to Profile → Account Management
    • Tap "Delete Account"
    • Confirm your decision in the dialog
    • Your account will be deleted immediately
  2. Email Request:
    • Send an email to: support@ejsstudios.com
    • Subject: "Account Deletion Request - Just Try-On"
    • Include your registered email address
    • Include your Google account email (if different)
    • We will process your request within 7 business days

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Profile Information: Name, email, profile picture
  • Generated Images: All AI-generated virtual try-on images
  • AI Model Configurations: Custom AI model settings and API keys
  • App Preferences: Theme settings, default configurations
  • Usage Statistics: Generation counts, streaks, and activity data
  • Account Authentication: Google Sign-In association

⚠️ Important Notice:

  • This action is permanent and cannot be undone
  • All your data will be permanently deleted from our servers
  • You will lose access to all generated images and configurations
  • If you want to use the app again, you'll need to create a new account
  • Data deletion is completed within 30 days of your request

Data Retention Policy

Upon account deletion:

  • Immediate: Account access is revoked immediately
  • Within 7 days: Personal data is removed from active systems
  • Within 30 days: All data is permanently deleted from backups
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Alternative: Data-Only Deletion

If you want to keep your account but delete your data, you can use the "Delete My Data" option in the app, which preserves your profile while removing:

  • Generated images
  • AI model configurations
  • App preferences and statistics
